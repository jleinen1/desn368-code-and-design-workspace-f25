French 75

The French 75 is a classic champagne cocktail with gin, lemon, and sparkling wine. This drink can be ordered at most restaurants, but Twigs Martini Bar is my favorite.

Ingredients

1 ounce gin

1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed

1/2 ounce simple syrup

3 ounces Champagne (or other sparkling wine)

Garnish: Lemon Twist

Instructions

Add the gin, lemon juice and simple syrup to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.

Strain into a Champagne flute.

Top with the Champagne or sparkling wine of choice.

Garnish with a lemon twist.

Variations

French 76: Swap gin for vodka for a cleaner, smoother sparkle.

Elderflower 75: Add a splash of St-Germain for a soft floral note.

Honey 75: Replace simple syrup with honey syrup for warmth and depth.
