French 75

The French 75 Wikipedia is a classic champagne cocktail with gin, lemon, and sparkling wine. This drink can be ordered at most bistros and restaurants in the Spokane area, but Twigs Martini Bistro is my favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • 3 ounces Champagne (or other sparkling wine)
  • Garnish: Lemon Twist
  1. Add the gin, lemon juice and simple syrup to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a Champagne flute.
  3. Top with the Champagne or sparkling wine of choice.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist.
  • French 76: Swap gin for vodka for a cleaner, smoother sparkle.
  • Elderflower 75: Add a splash of St-Germain for a soft floral note.
  • Honey 75: Replace simple syrup with honey syrup for warmth and depth.
