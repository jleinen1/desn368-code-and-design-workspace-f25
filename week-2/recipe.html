French 75

The French 75 is a classic champagne cocktail with gin, lemon, and sparkling wine. Itâ€™s light, refreshing, and perfect for celebrations!

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 ounce simple syrup
  • 3 ounces Champagne (or other sparkling wine)
  • Garnish: Lemon Twist
  1. Add the gin, lemon juice and simple syrup to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a Champagne flute.
  3. Top with the Champagne or sparkling wine of choice.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist.
