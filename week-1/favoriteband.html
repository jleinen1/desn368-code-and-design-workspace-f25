Car Seat Headrest

Car Seat Headrest is an American indie rock band led by singer-songwriter Will Toledo.
Originally a solo project started in 2010, Toledo began recording lo-fi albums in his car (hence the name) and self-releasing them on Bandcamp, gradually building a devoted online following.
